# What Paper 74 Does Not Claim

**Date:** March 2026

Per MASTER_SPEC_PAPER_74 §4.3:

## Explicit Non-Claims

1. **Phenomenology from O-SIAM alone** — Paper 74 does not prove phenomenology from Paper 73's operational boundary. Bridge premises are required.

2. **Extraordinary phenomena as empirical fact** — No establishment of anomalous efficacy or altering matter at a distance.

3. **Unrestricted selector access** — Access is bounded; relaxation has upper bounds.

4. **Interpretive consequences as formal conclusions** — Tier C content is interpretive only.

5. **Uniqueness** — Theory-space elimination, categoricity, and "uniquely selected survivor" belong to Paper 75. Paper 74 does not claim uniqueness.

## Forbidden Vocabulary

Do not use: siddhis, awakening, realization, anomalous efficacy, non-local efficacy, altering matter at a distance.

Use instead: selector-access restriction, restriction relaxation, access expansion, regime transition, etc.
